CURRENT ACCOUNT

A Current Account is a type of bank account mainly designed for businesses, companies, traders, and individuals who perform frequent financial transactions.

Features:
- Unlimited deposits and withdrawals.
- Suitable for business transactions.
- Provides cheque book facility.
- Internet and mobile banking available.
- Debit card is provided.
- Overdraft facility may be available for eligible customers.
- No interest is paid on the account balance.

Eligibility:
- Individual customers
- Proprietorship firms
- Partnership firms
- Private and Public Limited Companies
- Trusts and Societies

Documents Required:
- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Passport-size photographs
- Address Proof
- Business Registration Proof (for businesses)

Minimum Balance:
- Customers are required to maintain the minimum balance as specified by the bank.
- Charges may apply if the minimum balance is not maintained.

Benefits:
- Easy fund transfers using NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS.
- Quick payments through UPI and mobile banking.
- Secure online banking.
- SMS and Email transaction alerts.
- Monthly account statements.

Overdraft Facility:
Eligible customers can apply for an overdraft facility based on their relationship with the bank and financial eligibility.

Charges:
- Cheque book charges may apply after free limits.
- Non-maintenance of minimum balance may attract penalties.
- Other service charges are applicable as per bank policy.

Customer Support:
For any assistance, customers can contact the bank's customer care, visit the nearest branch, or use internet banking services.
